Katy has over 750+ hours of experience writing web apps, having completed over 20+ projects in both independent and Agile settings. When I'm not busy writing code, I enjoy trying new restaurants, playing chess, finding unique plant nurseries, gardening and exploring the beautiful North Carolina scenery.
